What is sex?

Sex is defined differently by many people. Different types of sex can include but is not limited too : Vaginal sex, Oral sex, Anal sex, and Masturbation.

How do I know when I'm ready to have sex?

First, there's no rush!It is totally ok to wait.Before you decide to have sex, ask yourself these questions:

  • Is sex something I really want, or am I being pressured in to it?
  • Is my partner someone I trust?
  • Am I okay with being vulnerable both emotionally and physically?
  • What are going to be the pros and cons of having sex? Focus on the impact of the cons before deciding
Sex can be great, but remember it's something only you can decide to do when you are ready. Remember to always use contraceptives! It protects you and your partner from getting STIs(HIV,Gonorrhoea,Chlamydia).The male condom also is 98% effective,therefore, your chances of getting pregnant are low.

How to practice safe sex?

When we are having sex, it's very important we do it safely. The are many differnt types of contraceptives we can use. The mostly commanly known contraceptive is the male condom. The male condom is 98% effective. Next, is the female condom. It is 95% effective. Both the male and female condom protect against STDs and prevent pregnancy. Another popular form of contraceptives are birth control pills. Birth control pills are oral contraceptives and they are also hormonal. Birth control pills don't protect you from STDs. Other forms of contraceptives are the patch, the ring, IUDs, and the shot. These are all hormonal contraceptives and like birth control pills, they aren't going to protect you from STDs
