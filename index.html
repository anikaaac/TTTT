Teenagers' Taboo Table Talk

Our Goal

Our mission at Teenagers' Taboo Table Talk is to provide information over topics that are considered taboo or unmentionable to teenagers. Plenty of teenagers nowadays are expected to learn about the importance of topics like sex education by themselves because schools do not provide adequate assistance for teenagers who are in need of resources and guidance. Along with that, many schools don't share enough resources with students over mental health and healthy relationships. Here at Teenagers' Taboo Table Talk, we have created a safe space in which teenagers can access information that is key as they transtion into adulthood. We not only provide information over the topics, we also have hotlines and other resources that are free and accessible to everyone.
