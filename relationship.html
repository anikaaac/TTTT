Healthy Relationships

What is and isn’t a healthy relationship?


A healthy relationship allow both partners to feel supported and connected but still feel independent. Respect for both oneself and others is a key characteristic of healthy relationships. In contrast, in unhealthy relationships, one partner tries to exert control and power over the other physically, sexually, and/or emotionally.

How to overcome hardships in relationships?


If your relationship is going through a rough patch you should try to first work on having a solid foundation of friendship with your significant other. If your both fighting acknowledge the situation and don’t deny, disregard or minimize what’s happening. Instead be honest and confont them whats bugging you. Then listen to each other more carefully, and show more curiosity about each other’s perspective, experience and needs.

What is Domestic Violence?


Domestiv Violence is a pattern of behaviors used by one partner to maintain power and control over another partner in an intimate relationship.It can happen to people who are married, living together or who are dating. It affects people of all socioeconomic backgrounds and education levels.


How to identify Domestic Violence and Abuse?


  • If one person in the relationship dominate and control the other
  • they use fear, guilt, shame, and intimidation to wear you down and keep you under their thumb
  • Any situation in which you are forced to participate in unwanted, unsafe, or degrading sexual activity
  • Prevents or discourages you from going to work or school or seeing family members or friends
  • Blames you for his or her violent behavior or tells you that you deserve it

    • If any of these apply to you please call this hotline
